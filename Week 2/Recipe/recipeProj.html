How to make a Matcha Latte!

Matcha, a special type of green tea, has a fascinating history that began in China during the Tang Dynasty. In the late 12th century, a Buddhist monk named Eisai introduced matcha to Japan, where it became popular for its ability to keep the mind focused during meditation. Over the years, matcha became an essential part of Japanese culture and tradition, especially in the tea ceremony. Its unique flavor, bright green color, and health benefits made it beloved worldwide, symbolizing harmony and mindfulness.

Matcha latte recipe from Food and Wine!

Ingredients:

  • Matcha Powder
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Ice

Tools:

  • Kettle
  • Bowl
  • Matcha Whisk
  • Sifter

Instructions:

  1. Acquire all tools and ingredients.
  2. Soak matcha whisk in warm water.
  3. Boil water in Kettle.
  4. Sift one teaspoon of Matcha powder into a glass bowl.
  5. Add sugar to the bowl.
  6. Pour boiling water into bowl.
  7. Whisk in a "W" motion until there is a bit of froth.
  8. Add Milk and Ice to a cup.
  9. Pour Matcha on top.
  10. Enjoy!
